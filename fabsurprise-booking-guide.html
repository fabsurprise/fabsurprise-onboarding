Things You Should Know

Every celebration is different, but here's what's helpful to know before we begin.

Your booking is confirmed once we receive your payment.

From that moment, we begin planning your celebration. Depending on your booking, we may start ordering items, booking vendors, preparing personalised gifts, and coordinating everything needed to bring it to life.

Full payment is our standard option for most bookings.

In select cases — larger celebrations or certain corporate projects — a payment plan may be agreed before work begins: 80% to confirm your booking and begin planning, with the remaining balance due before delivery, setup, or installation. This is the exception, not the default, and would always be discussed with you directly first.

Need to make a change? We'll always do our best to help.

If the change affects the cost, timeline, or items we've already ordered, we'll explain everything clearly before making any changes.

If your celebration date changes, let us know as soon as you can.

We'll do our best to move your booking to another available date. If extra costs are involved, we'll let you know before moving forward.

Plans change, and we understand. If you need to cancel, please let us know as soon as possible.

We'll look at what's already been arranged on our end — work completed, items ordered, vendors booked — before talking through the next steps with you together.

Every celebration is different, so refunds are considered individually rather than by a fixed formula.

If a refund is requested, here's what shapes the outcome:

  • Work already completed
  • Items already purchased
  • Personalised or custom-made pieces
  • Vendor and delivery costs already committed

If a refund is available, we'll walk you through exactly how it was calculated.

Some celebrations are very personal.

If you'd prefer we don't share photos or videos of your celebration on our website or social media, just let us know before the day. We'll always respect that.
